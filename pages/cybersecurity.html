Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage. It involves using tools, technologies, and rules to keep information safe from hackers and other threats. As more people and businesses use the internet, the risk of cybercrime increases. Cybersecurity helps prevent identity theft, data loss, and system failures. It includes things like using strong passwords, installing antivirus software, and keeping systems updated. Organizations also train employees to recognize phishing and scams. Governments and companies work together to build stronger defenses. Good cybersecurity keeps information private, secure, and available when needed.
